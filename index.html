About the Team

Who We Are

We are a student-led, VEX Robotics team located in Frisco, Texas, dedicated towards coding, building, and designing. This is our second year participating in VEX.

What We Do

We design, build, code a robot, and work as a team to compete at VEX tournaments. With the money funded, we hope to buy parts and improve our robot to help us win at tournaments.

How You Can Help

You can sponsor our team and support us financially. This will in turn help your brand as it provides you with national visibility, and promotes the next generation of engineers, coders, and designers.

The Challenge

Vex Push Back is a game where two alliances of two different teams compete to score points by placing blocks into goals, controlling zones within the goals, clearing match stations, and parking robots at the end of the match.
